Unit 1 – Session 2

Hour 1 · Grammar & Writing

Countries, nationalities & jobs

Objectives

  • Ask and answer: Where are you from?
  • Ask and answer: What do you do?
  • Use be in questions and short answers
  • Write a short profile of a real person

Warm-up

Think of three people you know.

  • Where are they from?
  • What do they do?

Share with a partner.

Grammar Focus

Questions with be

  • Where are you from?
  • I’m from Mexico.

  • Where is he from?
  • He’s from Brazil.

  • Where is she from?
  • She’s from Spain.

Jobs – Functional Questions

  • What do you do?
  • I’m a tour guide.

  • What does he do?
  • He’s a driver.

  • What does she do?
  • She’s a teacher.

Short Answers

  • Are you from Mexico? — Yes, I am. / No, I’m not.
  • Is he a guide? — Yes, he is. / No, he isn’t.
  • Is she from Canada? — Yes, she is. / No, she isn’t.

Controlled Practice

Complete the sentences:

  1. I ___ from Guatemala.
  2. She ___ a tour guide.
  3. They ___ not students.
  4. We ___ friends.
  5. He ___ from Italy.
  6. You ___ my classmate.

Mini Profiles

Write about someone you know:

  • Name
  • Country & nationality
  • Job
  • 1–2 extra details

Model Profile

“This is my colleague, Daniel. He is from Guatemala and he is Guatemalan.

He is a tour guide. He works with visitors from many countries.

He is very friendly and he loves his job.”

Writing Task

Write a 5–6 sentence profile in your notebook.

Then share with a partner.
